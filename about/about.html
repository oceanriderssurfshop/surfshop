Where it all began.

The OG's

Paulie Thoma

Marco Max

Davie Gregs

The OG Lahinch Ocean Riders, Paulie Thoma, Marco Max, and Davie Gregs, had been shredding the local breaks since they were groms, starting as tadpoles on the edge of the wave. After a perfect session where they were absolutely pumping on some massive swell, they realized their stoke for the ocean was too big to keep bottled up. They decided to combine their lifetime of wave knowledge into a surf shack where anyone could learn to catch a wave, from giving beginners a pop-up lesson to showing experienced surfers how to find the green room.

Meet The Team
