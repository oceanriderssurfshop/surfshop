Our Lesson Programs

Beginners

Learn the fundamentals of surfing, focusing on water safety, board control, and mastering the pop-up to ride your first whitewater waves toward the shore.

Intermediate

We progress beyond the broken waves by learning wave timing, paddling out efficiently, and steering the board to ride unbroken green waves parallel to the beach.

Advanced

Master high-performance maneuvers, speed generation, and ocean reading skills in powerful swell conditions to prepare for competitive surfing or large-wave expertise.
