Cookies Policy

This website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and help us improve our site.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when browsing a website. They help the site remember your preferences.

How We Use Cookies

- To remember your preferences (such as navigation choices)
- To understand how visitors use our website
- To provide basic functionality such as the weather widget

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage or block cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the site.
